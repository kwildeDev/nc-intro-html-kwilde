All About Oscar

Oscar is our lovable black and white cat, a domestic shorthair we adopted 7 years ago.

Favourite foods

Oscar tends to prefer Gourmet Perle in meat and fish flavours, so that is what we buy for him on repeat.

His favourite flavours are generally:

  • Tuna
  • Plaice and shrimp
  • Turkey

Favourite places to sleep

These are his favoured places to take a nap in order of preference (it also depends on how hot or cold it is that day):

  1. On B's bed
  2. On B's floor
  3. On my bed
  4. On the small toilet floor

Lovable traits

If you go into the back garden and call out "Oscar" or "Ozzy" you will often see him appear or jump down from somewhere, run towards you and miaow enthusiastically. He deserves a big fuss and/or some treats after that.

He enjoys getting on top of the water butt just outside the kitchen so he can paw at the kitchen window at us and miaow as if he can't get back in when there is a cat flap 2 metres away.

He loves sitting on the top of the settee cushions just behind people's heads.

Annoying habits

  • Scratching the settees.
  • Trying to get outside by jumping on the bathroom windowsill via the sink and leaving hairs everywhere.
  • Deciding that the best part of the furniture to sleep on is on top of my black trousers or blazer.

Footnote:

It can feel quite awkward using the small toilet when Oscar has taken to spending the day in there.
